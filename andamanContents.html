Detailed Itinerary:

Day 1:- Port Blair | Arrival and Sightseeing

As your arrive in the Andaman capital you will be greeted by the agent's representative and transferred to your hotel. Check in at your hotel and then proceed for a tour.


From there you will taken to the Corbyn Cove Beach which is fringed with coconut palms all over. You can swim, indulge in water sport activities at the beach or simply bask in the sun. Then visit Marine Park at Port Blair.


Then visit to the Cellular Jail in the evening to witness a Light and Sound Show which brings to life the story those who fought for our country against the British. Make your way back to the hotel for an overnight stay at port blair


Day 2:- Port Blair | Day tour of Ross & North Bay Islands

After breakfast, it will be time for a visit to famous Ross Island. Ross Island - the erstwhile capital of Port Blair during the British reign - now stands an imposing relic, with most of its structure almost in debris. A small museum on the island displays photographs and other antiques of the Britishers, relevant to these islands.


Later on, you will head towards North Bay Island. There, you can enjoy the view of colorful corals and underwater marine life through a glass-bottomed boat. Steal some solitary moments with as you trek up the Lighthouse.


From there, you will return to your hotel in Port Blair for dinner and night stay at port blair.


Day 3:- Havelock | Cruise to Havelock & Radhanagar Beach visit Havelock

Post breakfast you will board a jetty which takes you to the heavenly Havelock Island via private cruise. Upon arrival you will be transferred to for sightseeing at Havelock. You will visit the marvelous Radhanagar Beach which has been voted the best beach in Asia and is among the top ten beaches of the world.


After a memorable excursion at the beautiful beach, return to your hotel for an overnight stay.(havelock)


Day 4:- Havelock | Elephant Beach & lots of water activity

Elephant Beach is one of the most lovely beaches of Havelock Island with crystal clear water. It is famous for beach activities like snorkeling, Scuba. overnight stay.(Port Blair)


Day 5:- Port Blair | Departure

Savor a breakfast among the serene shores of Andaman before a transfer to the airport. Board your flight back home reminiscing about the wonderful memories you created on your trip.
